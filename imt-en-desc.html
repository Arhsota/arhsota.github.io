Description

 Metabolism is the chemical reactions in the body's cells that change food into energy. Our bodies need this energy to do everything from moving to thinking to growing. Specific proteins in the body control the chemical reactions of metabolism. Here we use equation of Harris-Benedict. The BMR value is your daily calories

  1.  Enter your weight in kg.
  2.  Enter your length in cm.
  3.  Enter your age. 
  4. Enter your style of life 
  5. Enter your gender 
  6. Click button CALCULATE.
  7. You will see digits on top, this is your BMI and BMR 
  8. BMI is index of your mass and BMR is level of calories per day
  9. Under the button CALCULATE, you will see a tip, what BMI is recommended in that ages. 
  10. For better view, under this tip, you will see visual bar.
